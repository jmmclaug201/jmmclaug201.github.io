Here, you’ll find all of my posts related to biking, which I find myself doing a lot of these days.

Links

Some places I find myself coming back to whenever I need biking inspiration:

  • Eric Zheng’s Cycling Blog, which I often read for ride ideas and which I copied was heavily inspired by in creating this blog.
  • Komoot, my go-to ride planning site.
  • Waymarked Trails, a comprehensive OpenStreetMap-based map of bike trails and routes.
  • Some Youtube Channels I like for motivation:
    • Paul Suchecki, who I especially like for his down-to-earth style as he bikes different trails around the United States.
    • Ryan Van Duzer, and especially his video on different biking routes across the United States.
  • Some resources for biking around Pittsburgh:
    • A comprehensive book of local routes (which I found via Eric Zheng’s blog).
    • A website with a bunch of links to various resources for biking in the area.
    • BikePGH, the local cycling organization.
